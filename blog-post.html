Bio

Wanderlust: a strong desire to travel.
Camping, traveling abroad and tiny house living may not be the norm for many like me, but I like to take the road "less traveled by, and that has made all the difference".

- Robert Frost, "The Road Not Taken"

Going against the grain is something I tend to do which is now leading me onto the path of programming despite it not being a field that many black women find themselves in. It's not always easy, but I'll crack the code. Pun intended.
